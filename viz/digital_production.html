Jannik Sinner defends his Australian Open title in a 3 set masterclass over Alexander Zverev

By Charlotte Che

Jannik Sinner, currently ranked 1st in the world, cruised past Alexander Zverev, currently ranked 2nd, to win his third grand slam title.

Amidst a long battle against doping allegations after testing positive twice for an anabolic banned steroid, clostebol, Sinner had a smooth run, overcoming top rising contenders like Australian Alex de Minaur, American Ben Shelton and Danish Holger Rune.
