About Me

Background

The best way I can think of describing myself is someone who loves the first day of school and classes -- new friends, new professors, new subjects, new things to learn.

I grew up in a small suburban town in northern New Jersey with two immigrant parents from South Korea and an older brother.

Some of my favorite things to do growing up were reading romance novels and watching the entire Marvel Cinematic Universe movies in chronological order (I've done this 3 times now!).
